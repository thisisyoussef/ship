# Spec-Driven Delivery

Use this workflow when the task needs design or pack-level planning without bypassing the checked-in story model.

## Canonical Sources

- `docs/plans/`
- `docs/specs/`
- `docs/user-stories/TEMPLATE.md`
- `docs/user-stories/README.md`
- `docs/submissions/`

## Rules

- Use `docs/plans/` for implementation thinking and design notes.
- Use `docs/specs/` for larger pack or phase technical plans that need durable structure.
- Use `docs/user-stories/TEMPLATE.md` and `docs/user-stories/README.md` to convert the work back into an executable story.
- Use `docs/submissions/` only for final review, demo, or submission bundles.

## Delivery Shape

1. capture the design in `docs/plans/` or `docs/specs/`
2. create or update the checked-in story that owns the implementation
3. execute the story through the normal validation and handoff flow
